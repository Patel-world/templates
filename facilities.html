Facilities and department

The college has the streams of education viz high school science, intermediate arts, Commerce, science - Mathematics as well as Biology.The college provides the best education by the renowned teachers.

Facilities

The college provides the important facilities which the students mostly need. The college has the facilities of NCC, scout, Red Cross as well as games. Lok Raj Sueaskar Is the CTO of NCC. Scout has two wings - Mr SS Shukla And ND Pandey Hold the command of it. The incharge of Red Cross or Mr Ramesh Kumar and Mr Vineet k. Shukla. The college is performing well in the field of games. Anurag Pandey and Rahul Yadav are the incharge of games.

Laboratories

The college has the physics lab, chemistry lab, Biology lab, military science lab, high school science lab, and geography lab.

All the modern equipment related to experiments are available in the hall of the Physics laboratory. In this all the facility of doing experiments for 80 students of intermediate is also available.

There is a system of lighting lamps from a gas plant in the Hall Of Chemistry laboratory. It contains a sufficient number of chemicals and balances. Simple microscope and Compound microscope are available in the Biology Laboratory. Here the two headed child safe in the jar and rare species of Snakes are the creatures of special attraction.

In the Western building of the school there is a military science laboratory for military studies. It has a seating arrangement for 60 students.

There is a high school science laboratory in room no. 23. Forty student can experiment in it simultaneously

There is also a geography laboratory in the western building of the school. It has a seating arrangement for 60 students.

Hostel facility

In the college there is also a hostel named Payagpur hostel ( PP hostel ). The foundation stone of this hostel was laid on on 15th May 1936 By the then district magistrate Shri H R Shivdarshan And was donated to the school as a donation by the then Raja of Payagpur state Shri Kunvar Surendra Vikram Singh. This hostel has 14 rooms and 1 hall. Shri Bhawani Shankar Mishra is Warden of this hostel.
